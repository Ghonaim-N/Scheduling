Availability Poll

This When2meet poll was created to coordinate our availability.

Please enter your full name. Enter a password if you would like the ability to return and modify your availability in the future. Clicking "Sign In" will bring you to an editable calendar. Time slots highlighted in green are available. Click and drag to select the times you're available for the event.

When2Meet Direct Link

The results for this poll will be used to find times to schedule an interview. Times will be chosen that best fit into all participants' schedules.
