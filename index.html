Availability Poll¶

Please fill out the When2meet poll using your full name.

Please enter your full name. Enter a password if you would like the ability to return and modify your availability in the future. Clicking "Sign In" will bring you to an editable calendar. Time slots highlighted in green are available. Click and drag to select the times you're available for the event. You can select multiple time slots by dragging over them.

  • When2Meet Direct Link

The results for this poll will be used to find times to schedule an interview. Times will be chosen that best fits into all participants' schedules.
